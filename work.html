Marco San Roman, AIA

The Architect

Professional, creative and innovative Licensed Architect with 15 years of experience working with well established firms as Hartshorne Plunkard Architecture, Tria Architecture, and Healy Bender Architects, designing high-rise, educational, commercial, and residential projects.

Credentials

Marco Graduated as an Architect from the University of Illinois in 2006.

Experience

Besides his acquired knowledged working with architecture firms, Marco has developed projects for restaurants, schools and residential real estates.

Marco has ample capabilites to manage a project through its development cycle from conception to full completion. He's attention to detail when complying with city codes, regulations and policies, will assure the project a successful fulfillment.
